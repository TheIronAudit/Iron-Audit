We audit gyms in North Yorkshire so you don’t waste your money.

Iron Audit is a community‑led scoreboard for real gym experiences in North Yorkshire. Instead of vague star ratings, we use a simple scorecard (busy at peak hours, cleanliness, equipment, facilities, staff, atmosphere, classes, and changing rooms) — so you can pick the right gym without guessing.

How it works

Structured scoring means less bias, more clarity — and fewer wasted memberships. Your vote helps everyone pick better gyms.

1) Rate fast

Submit a rating in under a minute. No essays, no drama — just a consistent scorecard.

2) See rankings

Gyms are ranked once they hit a minimum number of ratings, reducing one-person bias.

3) Filter by what matters

Later: find “least crowded”, “best value”, or “best for beginners” in your area.

4) Better gyms win

Gyms that improve move up. Members get better experiences and waste less money.

Fairness rule (recommended)

Only show a gym on the public leaderboard once it has 5+ ratings. One bad Monday doesn’t define a gym.

Freshness rule (recommended)

Weight recent ratings higher than old ones once you have enough data.
